Interactive horizon diagram. Press the left and right arrow keys to rotate the view horizontally, and the up and down arrow keys to change the viewing altitude. Page Up and Page Down change the viewing altitude in larger steps. Use the Star Position sliders, or the star handle, to move the star.

Use the arrow keys to move the star: left and right change its azimuth, up and down change its altitude. Hold Shift for finer steps; Page Up and Page Down change altitude in larger steps.
